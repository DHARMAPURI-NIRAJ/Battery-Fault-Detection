Telemetry Feature Dictionary
Voltage_Measured

Terminal voltage across the cell during discharge cycles (V).

Current_Measured

The flow of charge; used to calculate C-rate stability.

Temperature

Internal cell temperature (°C); primary driver of SEI layer growth.

Internal_Res

Ohmic resistance (Ω) indicating electrode degradation levels.

Cycle_Count

Number of full charge/discharge iterations performed.

Capacity (Target)

Maximum available charge (Ah); used for SoH calculation.
